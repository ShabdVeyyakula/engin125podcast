About Ethics in AI

From the ethical implications of artificial intelligence and data privacy to the impact of tech on the environment and human rights, "Ethics in AI" sheds light on how technological innovations can be aligned with ethical practices and responsible usage. Our discussions aim to enlighten, challenge, and inspire our audience to think critically about the role of technology in our future. Join us as we navigate the complexities of the tech landscape, uncovering the ways we can use technology to enhance our lives while ensuring it serves the greater good. Whether you're a tech enthusiast, a professional in the field, or just curious about how technology affects the world around you, "Ethics in AI" offers valuable perspectives and in-depth analysis to keep you informed and engaged.
